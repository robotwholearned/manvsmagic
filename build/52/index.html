The Good Old Days - Aug 13, 2014

A grandfather and his grandson are talking. The grandson asks “Grandpa, what was the internet like when you were a kid?”. The grandfather replies “Well, it was like an image caption contest anyone could enter but nobody won. Everything was up for debate and despite having the whole of human knowledge at their finger tips the debaters were always woefully unprepared. There was pornography and pictures of cats as far as you could scroll and people said horrible things for shock value in the name of free speech”. The grandson says “So, pretty much how it is now but without holograms?”. “Nah” the grandfather says “holograms made it weird”. Suddenly the hologram projector in front of them springs to life and a naked man with a cat head appears giving the middle finger with both hands yelling “Fuck you and your grandson you stupid old bitch!”.

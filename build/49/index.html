Hazard Pay - Aug 4, 2014

A newspaper headline that reads “CANADIAN MAN “MAKES IT RAIN” AT TORONTO STRIP CLUB: 3 DEAD, 17 INJURED”. Article written by Richard Cheese reads “TORONTO - In the aftermath of what can only be described as pandemonium 1 woman and 2 men lay dead after an overzealous patron of a popular Toronto gentlemen’s club began hurling fistfuls of loonies at the dancers on stage. Howard Peterson, who was among the 17 injured, spoke with reporters saying “I’ve never seen so many friggin’ loonies in my life”. Greg Jacobs, 38, was taken into police custody late last night and has been charged with involuntary manslaughter. The Canadian Prime Minister is expected to comment on the incident during a press conference later this afternoon. There will be a candle light vigil held outside The Hungry Beaver tomorrow evening starting at 9:00pm”. Accompanying the story is a photograph of a stripper crying and a man in a toque, mittens, and a sweater with a maple leaf on it howling in anguish with a tear running down his face. The photo is captioned “distraught witnesses”.

Cool Cops Play By The Rules - Sep 8, 2014

Two cops with amazing hair are standing outside their perps hideout. the first cop draws his weapon and says “Ivchenko’s holed up inside. It’s time we end this. I’m going in.” the second cop says “Maybe we should wait for backup to arrive”. The first cop holsters his gun and replies “yeah, I guess you’re right”. A while later after back up arrives and everyone is in handcuffs the two cops are looking on and the first cop says “Holy shit! We just about ran right into a trap! There must have been at least a hundred guys in there! Did you see those ninjas!?”, “What about that jacked Norwegian looking motherfucker?!” replies the second cop. “Well, I guess that’s why we have protocols.” says the first cop.

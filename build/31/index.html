Third Nipple - Jun 2, 2014

A man and a woman are on a first date and end up back at the man’s house. They’re making small talk. 'So, I showed you my toe with no toenail. What’s your weird thing?' says the woman. 'I have a third nipple' replies the man. 'Oh my God! No way! Show me!' exclaims the woman. The man lifts his shirt to reveal that he does indeed have a third nipple but also a malformed siamese twin that appears to be dead. 'Oh…Wow…Neat' the woman says trying to comprehend what she’s looking at. The man beams with pride still holding his shirt up. 'It’s getting late I should go' says the woman, unable to look away from the malformed baby sized twin. 'Maybe you should stay and I’ll show you my third testicle.' the man says obliviously. 'No, I want to go home now' the woman insists'. Suddenly the twin springs to life, reaches out his little hand and says 'Tag me in you’re blowing it!'.

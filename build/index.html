Like White Elephants - Sep 15, 2014

A doctor is counselling a female patient before surgery. The doctor says “It’s a perfectly simple procedure. You’ll be under conscious sedation, which means you’ll be awake but you won’t feel any pain. Afterwards, we’ll give you a prescription to manage any further pain or discomfort. Do you have any questions?”. Suddenly the woman bursts into tears and exclaims “I can’t do this doctor! I want to keep it”. The doctor explains “That’s because the tumor is actually putting pressure on your frontal lobe and affecting your judgement and reasoning.This will all make sense after the operation.”

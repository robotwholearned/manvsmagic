Jungle Love - Jun 16, 2014

A man is sitting at the bar when his friend approaches him. 'Hey man, I think that guy over there is hitting on your girlfriend.' says the friend. Indifferent the man says 'Whatever, I’m sure she’ll tell him off'. 'Whatever?! You’ve got to go assert your dominance!' shouts the friend. 'No, that’s weird. She’ll handle it. I trust her' replies the man. 'It’s not about trust.We’re animals, and right now that dude is blasting pheromones all over your girl!' insists the friend. 'Pheromones?' asks the man. 'Dude, it’s a jungle out there and you’ve got to mark your territory. Who’s the king of the jungle?' says the friend, 'the lion?' replies the man. 'Who’s the king of the jungle?!' shouts the friend, 'I am!' says the man. Suddenly we see the man urinating on his girlfriend who is charting up a handsome stranger.'What the fuck are you doing' says the girlfriend trying to comprehend the situation. 'I don’t know! I don’t know!' yells the man.

Fantastic Voyage - Sep 17, 2014

A doctor is explaining an exciting and experimental procedure to his male patient. The doctor says “We’ve discovered a way to actually miniaturize matter by shrinking individual atoms. I’d like to shrink a vessel and crew and send them inside your body to remove the aneurysm”. The man excitedly replies “That’s amazing Doc! Absolutely! Whatever it takes!”. The doctor holding up a rather phallic looking vessel the size of a cucumber replies “Excellent! We’ve already made preparations. The ship and crew have been miniaturized and are ready to disembark”. The man trepidatiously replies “Doc, that’s still pretty big. How is this supposed to work? I don’t know about this”. The doctor seemingly irritated by the man’s objections says quite sternly “Listen you little ship tease, I said the crew’s ready to disembark and that’s what they’re going to do.”

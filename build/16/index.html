Oh, The Places We'll Go - Mar 24, 2014

A man wearing virtual reality goggles that look like the oculus rift is telling his friend all about how wonderful they are. He says 'I’m telling you man, they’ve finally got this virtual reality stuff figured out. This thing is amazing! It’s so immersive. Imagine the applications! Gamers exploring fantastic imaginary worlds, medical students standing inside of damaged organs, experiencing daily life in another person’s shoes, attending classes or the workplace as a genderless, raceless avatar where only your mind and the content of your character are judged!'. The friend asks 'so what are you using it for?', it’s now apparent that the man wearing the virtual reality headset has a visible erection and he says 'surprisingly, just porn so far'.

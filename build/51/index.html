Dogpocalypse - Aug 11, 2014

A T.V. News anchor is reporting on a story. “Day 5 of what officials are now calling ‘Dogpocalypse’. The giant pack of dogs continues to grow as formerly domesticated dogs turn on their owners to join the pack. We now go live to Doug Turner for more on this developing story”. Cut to Doug Turner saying “Thanks Carol. I’m standing at ground zero, the dog park where the pack first formed. Investigators are still trying to figure out how something like this could have happened”. The scene cuts to 5 days earlier where to men are talking. “Explain to me what we’re doing again?” says the first man. The second man holding a large loudspeaker says “We’re going to play a recording of wolves howling over this loudspeaker down at the dog park, like those Youtube videos but with more dogs. It’ll be funny”.

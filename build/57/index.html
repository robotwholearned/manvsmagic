What’s My Motivation? - Sep 1, 2014

James Bond is being held captive by Goldfinger. James asks “do you expect me to talk?” Goldfinger replies “No, mommy, I expect you to die”. Goldfinger thinks for a moment and realizes he misspoke, James Bond smirks “I mean Mr.Bond” Goldfinger corrects himself, Bond is still smiling. Goldfinger suddenly becomes choked up and his eyes well up. He turns away as a single tear rolls down his cheek. “Come on Goldfinger. Not in front of Bond” he says to himself.

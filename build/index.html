Academic Misconduct - Aug 27, 2014

A grey haired man in a suit who appears to be a school official of some sort is sitting at his desk addressing two unseen students. “As I’m sure you both know, we take academic misconduct very seriously here.We have zero tolerance in cases of plagiarism. Any and all parties involved face immediate expulsion and a black mark on their permanent records. So, perhaps one of you would like to explain the striking similarities between your most recent assignments”. The man holds up two pieces of paper that have similar looking stick figures on them and the words mommy scrawled across the bottom in crayon. The two students, who appear to be kindergarteners respond “Uh oooooh”. “Uh oh indeed” responds the grey haired man.

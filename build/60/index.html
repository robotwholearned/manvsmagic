Final Wishes - Sep 10, 2014

An old man is laying on his death bed with his loyal butler tending to him, the butler is clearly sad. "Sinclair, I'm dying. These are my final instructions. For all your years of loyal service, for allowing me dignity during my time of illness and in death I bequeath to you . . . Any ship of your choosing from my model ship collection. Except for the HMS Victory." Sinclair eyes widen, he looks as though he is incensed but holding back his anger, he remains silent, the old man continues "As for my fortune, I wish to be buried with my money. Any and all proceeds from the sale of my estate are to be burned in front of the poor." The old man dies. Sinclair's brow furrows as he wonders how to slight the dead man. In the next scene we see the old man in his underwear hanging by a noose, with a ball gag in his mouth, and one hand in his underwear holding his crotch. A police officer on the scene comments "It's just never enough for these rich old bastards."

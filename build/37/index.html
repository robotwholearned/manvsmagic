Trying To Explain Gang Signs For The First Time - Jun 23, 2014

Two gang members are having a conversation. 'So, they’ve got to do the thing with their fingers or they can’t be in the gang?' asks the first gang member. 'No, they don’t have to do it to get in the gang, you just do it if you’re in the gang.' replies the second. 'Right, and if you can’t do it you’re out of the gang.' says the first, chucking a thumb over his shoulder. 'No, it’s just, like, a sign' corrects the second gang member. 'A sign of what?' asks the first. 'The gang' replies the second. 'I feel like we’re talking in circles' says the first.

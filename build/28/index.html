Topical Stuff - May 21, 2014

Two men who appear to be assistants to Michael Jackson are having a conversation. The first man says 'MJ’s out of control. We’ve got to do something!' and the second man says 'don’t you think I know that?! But he’s the King of Pop! He's a genius! The man made Thriller for Christ sake!' to which the first man replies 'genius or not, we’ve got to rein him in. No more Jesus juice, no more shopping sprees, and no more sleepovers!', then the second man replies 'might I remind you that he wrote Black or White during one of those sleepovers!'. Suddenly they’re interrupted by Michael Jackson. 'Oh hey Mike' says the first man. Michael Jackson says 'Idiot one, I had another rough sleepover last night, I need you to smooth things over with some angry parents. Idiot two, get me Paul Mcartney’s new number. Me and some Make-A-Wish kids are going to get fucked up and make some prank calls. I’m going to go record some hits. Later dummies', Michael Jackson turns and leaves. We cut to a man and woman trying to decide what to watch on TV and find that the scene with the assistants and Michael Jackson was simply a metaphor for why the man didn’t want to watch House M.D. '…and that’s what it’s like' says the man. 'Fine' says the woman 'but we’re not watching Scarface again'.
